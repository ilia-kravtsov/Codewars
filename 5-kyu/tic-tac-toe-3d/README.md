<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/5aa67541373c2e69a20000c9/train/javascript target="_blank">Tic-Tac-Toe (3D)</a></h2><h3>5 kyu</h3><p>Regular Tic-Tac-Toe is boring.</p><p>That's why in this Kata you will be playing  Tic-Tac-Toe in <strong>3D</strong> using a 4 x 4 x 4 matrix!</p><img title="source: imgur.com" src="https://i.imgur.com/NlG3wai.png"><h1 id="kata-task">Kata Task</h1><p>Play the game. Work out who wins.</p><p>Return a string</p><ul><li><code>O wins after &lt;n&gt; moves</code></li><li><code>X wins after &lt;n&gt; moves</code></li><li><code>No winner</code></li></ul><h1 id="rules">Rules</h1><ul><li>Player <code>O</code> always goes first</li><li>Input <code>moves</code> is list/array/tuple of <code>[x,y,z]</code> (zero based)</li><li>Each player takes a turn until you find a winner, or there are no moves left</li><li>Four of the same symbols in a row wins</li><li>There may be more moves provided than are necessary to finish the game - that is for you to figure out</li></ul><hr style="border-bottom 1px solid white"><span style="color:orange">Good Luck!<br>DM</span>
