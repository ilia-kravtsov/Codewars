<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/5c2d5de69611562191289041/train/javascript target="_blank">Kata 2019: Bonus Game I</a></h2><h3>5 kyu</h3><h1 id="task">Task</h1><p>John won the championship of a TV show. He can get some bonuses.</p><p>He needs to play a game to determine the amount of his bonus.</p><p>Here are some cards in a row. A number is written on each card.</p><p>In each turn, John can take a card, but only from the beginning or the end of the row. Then multiply the number on the card by an coefficient 2<sup>i</sup>(i means the i<sub>th</sub> turn). The product is John's bonus of current turn.</p><p>After all the cards are taken away, the game is over. John's final bonus is the sum of all rounds of bonuses.</p><p>Obviously, the order in which John takes the cards will affect the amount of John's final bonus.</p><p>Your task is to help John calculate the maximum amount of bonuses he can get.</p><h1 id="input">Input</h1><ul><li><p><code>cards</code>: An integer array. Each element represents the number on the card.</p><ul><li><code>1 &lt;= cards.length &lt;= 30</code></li><li><code>1 &lt;= cards[i] &lt;= 100</code></li></ul></li><li><p>All inputs are valid.</p></li></ul><h1 id="output">Output</h1><p>An integer. the maximum amount of bonuses John can get.</p><h1 id="eaxmple">Eaxmple</h1><p>For <code>cards=[1,2,5]</code>, the output should be <code>50</code>.</p><pre><code>All possible orders are:1-&gt;2-&gt;5   bonus:1x2+2*4+5*8=501-&gt;5-&gt;2   bonus:1x2+5*4+2*8=385-&gt;1-&gt;2   bonus:5*2+1*4+2*8=305-&gt;2-&gt;1   bonus:5*2+2*4+1*8=26The maximum amount of bonus is 50.</code></pre>
